Description

Apartments 200 m from the sea in a quiet area of Kargicak.
Construction will begin at the end of April 2022, with completion scheduled for December 2023. During the construction phase there is an interest-free installment plan, 30% down payment.

Kargicak is a picturesque area, which is located just 3 km from the Mahmutlar district, 16 kilometers from the city of Alanya. The village is surrounded on all sides by picturesque forests. On one side of it are the Toros Mountains and on the other side the coastal strip of the Mediterranean Sea, which stretches for 4.5 kilometers.

Nearby is the main street and embankment, where there are supermarkets, a food and clothing market, a beautiful embankment, sports and children's playgrounds, a park and recreation area, beaches equipped for swimming and restaurants right on the seashore, and the neighboring large district of Mahmutlar.

On an area of 1,743 m2 there are 2 blocks of 5 floors, a lobby, an outdoor pool, a children's outdoor pool, an indoor heated pool, a relaxation area with sun loungers, a sauna, a Roman steam room, a gym, a room for Pilates and yoga, and parking.
Available layouts: 1+1 with an area of 55 m2, 2+1, 3+1.

The apartments are offered in a standard fine finish with a built-in kitchen unit with granite countertops. The apartments will be equipped with furniture and plumbing fixtures in the bathrooms, with the installation of a VEITO instantaneous water heater. The design of the apartments will be decorated in light and gray colors. The actual ceiling height is 3 m. Flooring: porcelain stoneware in the living room and bathrooms, and laminate in the bedroom. New apartments in a quiet area of Kargicak, close to the sea, the airport, the center of Alanya, under construction, for investment, cheaper than the market price.
